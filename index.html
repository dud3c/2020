Escape Grandpa
Available on android, Escape Grandpa is a first person puzzle game with a presence of horror based on the app "Granny".The goal is to escape the house through various puzzles, using tools hidden around the house in random locations such as draws, bushes and shelves, while using stealth to hide from Grandpa. The player can choose to play easy, medium or hard, and select lights on/off to control the level of horror
